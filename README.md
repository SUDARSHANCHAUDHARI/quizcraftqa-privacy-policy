# Privacy Policy for QuizCraftQA

This repository contains the privacy policy for [QuizCraftQA](https://github.com/SUDARSHANCHAUDHARI/QuizCraftQA) — an ISTQB exam preparation web app that turns PDF study materials into adaptive quizzes.

## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 26, 2026**

### Overview

QuizCraftQA is a client-side web application. All core functionality runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- No user accounts or registration
- No cookies or tracking
- No analytics or telemetry
- No personal information of any kind

### PDF Processing

All PDF files you upload are processed **locally in your browser** using pdf.js. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

### Local Storage

Quiz history, progress, streaks, and settings are stored in your browser's **IndexedDB** (local to your device). This data never leaves your device.

### Optional AI Features

QuizCraftQA includes optional AI features (study plan generation, question variants). These are **disabled by default** and only activate if you configure your own API endpoint via environment variables.

If you enable an AI endpoint, your quiz content may be sent to that endpoint. That endpoint's own privacy policy applies. QuizCraftQA itself does not operate any AI backend.

### Third-Party Services

QuizCraftQA does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

### Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this policy will be reflected in this repository with an updated date.

### Contact

For questions, open an issue at [github.com/SUDARSHANCHAUDHARI/QuizCraftQA](https://github.com/SUDARSHANCHAUDHARI/QuizCraftQA).
